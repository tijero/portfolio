The Context

A new revolution knocks at the door of our society, the industry is undergoing its fourth metamorphosis and this time the robots and the data are the protagonists of this transformation. The world as we know it is numbered days, the convergence of digital, physical and biological technologies will define the future of employment in our society.

The Problem

The Problem

Up to 800 million people (or 30% of the global workforce) will see their jobs at risk and will have to look for a new job in the near future because of automation.

The Context The Research

The Research

The most susceptible jobs to be replaced by a machine are those that involve physical work and those that occur in predictable environments, such as operating machinery or preparing fast food. We propose the two most susceptible scenarios to undergo the change with real employees.
